Chicken Fried Rice

Description

Indulge your taste buds in a symphony of flavors with our exquisite Chicken Fried Rice recipe. This Asian-inspired dish is a harmonious blend of tender chicken, fluffy rice, and an array of vibrant vegetables, creating a mouthwatering fusion that's both comforting and satisfying.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 onion
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 115g frozen peas
  • 250g cooked chicken
  • 1/2 tbsp sesame oil
  • 800g boiled and cooled long-grain-rice
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tbsp dark soy sauce
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • juice of 1/2 lemon

Steps:

  1. Add 1 tbsp of the oil in a large wok. Add the onion and cook, stirring regularly on a medium heat for 5 minutes until the onion starts to turn soft and translucent.
  2. Add in the remaining vegetable oil, chopped red bell pepper, garlic, and peas. Cook for a further 2 minutes, stirring regularly.
  3. Now add chicken and sesame oil. Stir together, then add in the rice and turn up the heat to high.
  4. Use a spatula to toss everything together and to ensure the rice doesn't stick to the bottom of the pan. Keep moving the rice around so that it's all getting reheated.
  5. Once the rice is hot (this will take about 5 minutes), push the rice over to the side of the pan and crack the two eggs in the space. Add a little splash of the soy sauce to the eggs.
  6. Making sure the part of the wok with the eggs in is over the heat, fry the eggs, giving a mix with the spatula until the egg starts to cook and looks scrambled, but is still a little runny.
  7. Now mix the egg into the rice.
  8. Pour in the remaining soy sauce, sprinkle on the salt and mix it all together.
  9. Add the lemon juice, stir and taste. Add a little more lemon juice if required.
  10. Divide between 4 bowls. Top with spring onions (scallions).
